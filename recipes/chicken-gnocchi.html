Chicken Gnocchi Soup



Ingredients
~2 Lbs of Whatever Chicken You Want
2 tbsp Olive Oil
3 tbsp butter
1 tbsp minced garlic
1 cup diced onion (about 1 whole onion)
1/4 cup flour
2 tbsp cornstarch
3 cups Chicken Stock
4 cups Whole Milk
1 Bay Leaf
1 tsp chicken boullion
1/2 tsp paprika
1/2 tsp oregano
1/2 tsp thyme
1/2 tsp black pepper
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup shredded carrots
1 cup packed spinach
16 oz Potato Gnocchi

Tools
Large Pot
Small Bowl
Measuring Cups/Spoons
Spoon/Ladle
Whisk

Instructions- Prep
Whisk together:
4 Cups Whole Milk
2 Tablespoons Cornstarch
Set aside for later.

Prepare your chicken however you want or need as long as it gets into small pieces and is cooked fully.

Instructions- Toss Things Into a Pot
Stir together 3 Tablespoons Butter and 2 Tablespoons Olive Oil into the pot over medium heat until the butter is fully melted.
Add 1 Cup Diced Onions and stir for about 5 minutes, or until a little brown.
Add 1 Tablespoon Minced Garlic and 1/4 Cup Flour and stir for about 2 minutes.

Turn heat to low, add 3 Cups Chicken Stock.
Add Cornstarch/Milk Mixture, all seasonings, and 1 Teaspoon Chicken Boullion.
Add Chicken, 1 Cup Packed Spinach, 1 Cup Shredded Carrots, and cook until the spinach is fully cooked (it will look shrivled).
Add Gnocchi and cook until they are tender and floating.
Remove bay leaf and serve!
