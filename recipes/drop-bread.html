Drop Biscuits



Ingredients
2 Cups Flour
1 Stick / 8 Tablespoons Cold Unsalted Butter
1 Tablespoon Baking Powder
1 Teaspoon Kosher Salt
1 Cup Milk

Tools
Oven
Bowl
Food Processor/Hands
/Something to Mix Real Good
Parchment Paper
Baking Sheet

Instructions- Everything
Preheat oven to 400 F.

Crumble together very well:
2 Cups Flour
1 Stick Butter
1 Tbsp Baking Powder
1 Tsp Kosher Salt

Put into a bowl and add 1 Cup Milk and stir.
Drop chunks onto a parchment-lined baking sheet and bake 18-20 minutes. Makes 6 big biscuits.
