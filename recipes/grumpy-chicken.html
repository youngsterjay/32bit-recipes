Grumpy Chicken



Ingredients
4 Bone-in, Skin-on Chicken Thighs
1/2 Stick of Butter, cut into 4ths
Garlic Powder
Onion Powder
Chives
Parsely
Salt
Pepper

Tools
Oven
Baking Sheet
Wire Baking Rack (not necessary)
Paper Towels/Something to Dry

Instructions- Everything
Preheat oven to 375 F.

Thoroughly dry chicken thighs.
Place on baking sheet/wire baking rack.

Place one knob of butter beneath the skin of each chicken thigh.
Sprinkle generously with all the seasonings. (The salt makes it grumpy)

Bake 35-45 minutes until internal temperature reaches 165F. Put into a bowl and add 1 Cup Milk and stir.
