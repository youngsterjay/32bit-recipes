Fruity Oaty Bars



Ingredients
200g Dates (Stones Removed)
100g Coconut Oil
150ml Water
1 tsp Cinnamon
Pinch of Salt
150g Oats
50g Raisins
50g Cashews
50g Chocolate Chips
the last four ingredients can be 300g of anything)

Tools
Pot
Bowl
Wooden Spoon

Instructions- Everything
Put 200g Dates into a pot of water over low heat.
Use a wooden spoon to press the dates into the sides of the pan so they melt.
Add the 100g Coconut Oil and melt it as well.
In a bowl, mix together all other ingredients.
Turn off the heat and pour the dry ingredients in slowly, mixing as you go.
Keep going until the consistency is okay; err on the side of too dry, because too much date flavoring will make it weird.
Pour onto a flat surface and cool.
Cut into small squares.
You're all done! This recipe is from Casimir, who can be found here!
